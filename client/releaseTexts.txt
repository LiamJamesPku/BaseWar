Version 3.3 (previous Version: 3.2):

Features:
* Added difficulty level (easy, normal, hard, very hard, hell) for AI player; before version 3.3 AI was set to normal
* Added mana to units. Skills will now be trigged with mana instead of a cool down
* Added possibility to upgrade mana & mana regeneration
* Added possibility to upgrade skills

Other changes:
* Improved armor upgrade. (Now it is -5% damage taken per upgrade)
* Changed upgrade of attack damage, run speed and hit points to (1.15^upgradeNum) * hitpoints [previously: (1 + (upgradeNum*0.15)) * hitpoints ]

GUI improvements:
* Unitinfo menu (menu to the right) is renamed to info menu
* Improved usability of the info menu
* Added a section "button info" to the info menu. If you tap on a button in this section you will see its behaviour.
* New health bar design
* Added game tips at loading screen
* Units with skills will have a mana bar under the health bar
* Added the possibility to hide the health bar

For balancing / unit changes, please see the forums: http://www.plankes-projects.com/basewar/forum/viewtopic.php?f=9&t=47&p=86

Version 2.5 (previous Version: 2.3):

Features:
* Added game statistics feature: number of played/won/lost games is recorded for different game types (local, local vs. human, online)
* BaseWar now requires the player to have won at least two games against the AI before playing online battles
* The tower and the castle may not be healed by units any more. Instead, they regenerate health points on their own.

Fixes:
* Displaying indicator if online player is AI or human
* Improved buy menu behavior
* Fixed in-game menu open/close animation speed
* Fixed some spelling and typo mistakes in the menus

Änderungen:
* Spielstatistiken hinzugefügt: die Anzahl der gespielten/gewonnenen/verlorenen Spiele wird für die verschiedenen Spielvarianten aufgezeichnet
* Um BaseWar Online zu spielen, müssen nun zumindest 2 Spiele gegen den Computer gewonnen werden.
* Der Turm und die Burg können nun nicht mehr von Einheiten geheilt werden. Dafür regenerieren sie sich langsam selbst.

Verbesserungen:
* Es wird nun bei Online-Spielen angezeigt, ob der Spieler ein Computergegner oder ein menschlicher Spieler ist
* Performance des Kaufmenüs wurde verbessert
* Animation des Kaufmenüs für unterschiedliche Bildschirmgrößen angepasst
